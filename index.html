About Me

I live and work in Madrid, Spain. I am a Telecommunications Engineer from the University Carlos III of Madrid, and since my end of degree project I have been dedicated to developing applications.

I am a proactive person and I strongly believe in teamwork as a basis to achieving excellent results in a project. Moreover, I am very interested in the agile software development methodologies.
